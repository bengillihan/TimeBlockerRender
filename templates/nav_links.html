  • Office 365 Calendar:
    1. Go to Outlook Calendar
    2. Click "Settings" (gear icon) > "View all Outlook settings"
    3. Go to "Calendar" > "Shared calendars"
    4. Under "Publish a calendar", select your calendar
    5. Click "Publish" and choose "Can view all details"
    6. Copy the IframePub URL (ends with "...&embed=true")
  • Custom Iframe Code:
    1. Copy your embed code from the source website
    2. Paste it in the "Custom Iframe Code" field
    3. The code should look like: <iframe src="..." width="100%" height="600" ...>
